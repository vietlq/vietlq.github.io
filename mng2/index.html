Resume & Candidate Search Optimisation (CSO)

  1. Keep resume/CV short and sweet: 1 page, 2 pages max
  2. Tell nothing but TRUTH
  3. Only relevant experience
  4. List quantifiable, measurable achievements/contributions: Sped up the system by X%; Reduced memory usage by Y times; Cut the monthly usage by $Z; Increased throughput K times, ...
  5. No personal information for discrimination: Sex, age, height, weight, blood type, photo, country of origin, home address, religion, hobbies, ...

  5. Did well in the past interviews

Best Practices During Interviews

  1. STOP!!! Ask enough questions before coding!
  2. Discuss solution before coding! Try to gather hints and feedback to optimise your solution
  3. Think out loud, especially during phone & video interviews. Show your thought process
  4. Always check for edge cases and bugs! They are deal-breakers!
  5. Maintain enough eye contact and speak slowly, clearly to project confidence
  6. Always attempt to solve, never give up!

Best Practices During Interviews

Minor Points

  1. Don't ask for feedback from the interviewers, there are legal implications and interviewers might be instructed not to give direct or indirect feedback
  2. Prepare a few questions to ask about company culture, technologies, challenges, directions of the company. Show genuine interest in the company and the team
  3. Get enough sleep and food before interviews
  4. Ask for dress code, look good and smell good

Hiring Process (Popular)

  1. Recruiter screening: Basic Big-O questions, quick bites on data structures, algorithms
  2. Phone screening (30-60 mins)
  3. Hangout / Skype / Nexi / Video conference (45-90 mins)
  4. On-site: 2-4 rounds of technical interviews (45-60 mins each)
  5. HR / Manager round
  6. Feedback, Offer or Rejection
  7. Background check

Hiring Process (Mostly financial companies)

  1. Online Psychometric (Verbal, Numerical) tests, behavioral questions
  2. Online in-depth programming language, OS tests: C/C++, Java, Unix, Linux, Windows, concurrency, multi-threading, kernel, dynamic memory allocation, ...
  3. Homework that takes 3-6 hours, mostly for experienced, senior positions
