One of my top priorities when installing a system is to make it home by installing Vietnamese input and fonts. There are a few options to install Vietnamese input. I got accustomed to Telex input style so I'll cover it here. Screenshots were taken from Leopard but installing on Tiger is the same. Here are the steps:

  1. Download from Vietnamese-2.0.1.dmg</em></strong> (or any later version available) http://homepage.mac.com/herr/</a></li>
  2. Do normal installation (see attached screenshots)</li>
  3. Log out as shown on the screenshot</li>
  4. Log in and go to System Preferences, Click on International</li>
  5. And then choose the Right-most tab "Input Menu"</li>
  6. Scroll down and find "Vietnamese Telex", tick it</li>
  7. Close the System Preferences</li>
  8. From the menu bar, choose Vietnamese and enjoy typying:</li>
    </ol>
